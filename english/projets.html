RETROS arcade cabinet

It is a prototype of an interactive arcade cabinet made on Figma, specially designed for retro game lovers. Present at Made in Asia or Japan Expo conventions, this cabinet allows you to test a variety of retro games before buying them. And that’s not all: you can also order retro consoles like Sega, Nintendo, Playstation, and more. With this cabinet, relive your childhood memories and discover new exciting games.

Application SOLICAR

Lors de ma formation en web design, j’ai fait équipe pour créer un prototype d’application de partage de covoiturage spécialisé pour les villages en Belgique. Avec cette application, les utilisateurs peuvent créer des groupes et partager des voyages en utilisant des véhicules électriques loués. Cela réduit non seulement les coûts de transport, mais aussi l’impact environnemental. En plus de l’application mobile, nous avons développé un site Web et une interface de tableau de bord pour faciliter la gestion des voyages et des groupes. Nous sommes convaincus que cette solution de covoiturage peut apporter de réels bénéfices aux communautés locales en Belgique.

Interface for ice-cream dispenser

This prototype touch interface is intended to be integrated into self-service ice cream dispensers, allowing customers to customize their own ice cream. With an easy-to-use touchscreen, customers can choose their ice cream scent and add toppings. The intuitive interface also allows customers to view ice cream as they order, giving them a clear picture of their creation. This prototype was designed to provide a smooth and enjoyable user experience, while improving product customization and meeting customer expectations.

Personal website Cardrage.be

CARDRAGE is a true showcase of the beauty of the automobile through photoshoots and high quality video clips. The purpose of this site is to capture the essence of these unique and beautiful cars and present them in their best light through photography and video. Thanks to the clear and intuitive design of this site, visitors can easily navigate between the different shootings and video clips and discover the diversity of this portfolio. Whether it’s sports cars, luxury cars or classic cars, every video clip and every photo is a true work of art that pays tribute to the automobile.
